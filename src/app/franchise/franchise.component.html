LARGEST RESTAURANT CHAIN OF INDIA

First Fiddle Restaurants, one of the india’s largest F&B company in the casual dining sector, is headed by Priyank Sukhija. From Lazeez Affair, the first brand conceived in 1999 to Dragonfly Experience and Plum By Bent Chair, its latest ventures, it has brought a wave of innovation in the hospitality industry. It has been lauded with an endless number of awards by ‘Times Nightlife’, ‘GQ’, ‘Indian Restaurant Awards’- to name a few. With more than 35 brands under its name such as Lord Of The Drinks, Tamasha, The Flying Saucer Café, JLWA, it has grown manifold in the past 20 years and continues to do so.
